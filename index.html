Dave Threlkeld

Web Developer

Dave Threlkeld is a web developer studying at the University of Texas at Austin. His professional background is predominately project management in the last decade and various technical positions in the decade before that. Starting out as a jazz major at University of North Texas, he became an accomplished sound engineer and worked through the ranks, eventually becoming a talent buyer presenting concerts in the Northwest United States. Parallel to the professional side he volunteered as an EMT/Firefighter in college and the hazmat training became a full time profession during the BP oil spill, leading a team of HAZWOPERS in the worst affected areas. After the oil spill he moved back to Texas to work on a career in management and joined a local volunteer fire department, rising to his current rank of Assistant Chief. The Covid pandemic illustrated the volatility of non-tech contract projects and he enrolled at UT to study web development. He remains very involved in the community music scene, playing in Austin's Minor Mishap Marching Band.
